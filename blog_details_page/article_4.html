What’s New in Dermal Fillers for 2025

Prepared by Dr Woo Fui Teng

Natural Results, Longer Lasting Effects

Introduction

Dermal fillers continue to evolve—and 2025 is the year of more natural, personalized, and longer-lasting enhancements. Whether you're curious about your first filler treatment or you're a regular looking for the latest updates, here's what to expect in the new era of facial rejuvenation.

1. Biostimulatory Fillers Are Taking Center Stage

In 2025, we’re seeing a surge in biostimulatory fillers like Sculptra® and Radiesse®, which stimulate collagen for gradual, long-term improvement.

Pictures showing before and after Sculptra treatment.


2. Longer-Lasting HA Fillers, Less Swelling

One of the biggest advancements in dermal fillers for 2025 is the rise of longer-lasting hyaluronic acid (HA) fillers with minimal downtime—and Definisse® Fillers are leading this shift. Thanks to their innovative XTR™ Technology, Definisse fillers offer enhanced stability and longevity, often lasting up to 18 months while maintaining a soft, natural feel. The ultrapure, Swiss-made HA used in Definisse also means reduced risk of swelling and post-treatment reactions, making it a favorite for patients who want effective, low-maintenance results. This new-generation filler allows for precise, expressive enhancements with less need for frequent touch-ups or recovery time.


3. Skin-Quality Fillers for a “Filtered” Glow

Not all fillers are about volume. Skin boosters like Profhilo, Hydrobooster, NCTF, Plinest and Rejuran focus on hydration and texture—great for achieving that "glass skin" glow.



4. The "Invisible Touch" Aesthetic

2025’s trend is subtle: micro-dosing and layering techniques mean you look refreshed—not “done.” The goal? Look like you, on your best day.
