Thinking of Enhancing Your Skin But Feeling Unsure?

You're not alone. Many first-time clients feel nervous before their initial aesthetic consultation. Whether you're considering skin rejuvenation, anti-aging treatments, or acne solutions, knowing what to expect can help you walk in feeling confident—and walk out one step closer to your dream skin.



Before the Consultation: How to Prepare

You don’t need to do much before your consultation, but here are a few simple tips to get the most out of your visit:

  • Know your skincare products or keep a list on your phone. This helps our doctors and beautician to understand your daily routine.
  • Know your concerns and goals. Whether it's dull skin, fine lines, acne or you just want a skin rejuvenation. Unsure? Don’t worry, our team will help you to find the best treatment for your concern.
  • Avoid makeup if possible—but fret not, we will help remove your makeup before the consultation to ensure accurate recommendations and assessment.

During the Consultation: A Safe, Judgment-Free Zone

Here’s a breakdown of what typically happens during your first aesthetic consultation at Aessence/Cantiq clinic.

1. Skin Analysis

We’ll begin with a professional skin assessment by our doctors to observe your concerns—like dehydration, pigmentation, or clogged pores.



2. Discussion of Concerns & Goals

This is your time to talk! We’ll ask about your:

  • Skincare routine
  • Lifestyle (stress, sleep, water intake)
  • Treatment goals (e.g. glow, lifting, anti-acne)

You can also ask questions about pricing, downtime, and results—nothing is off limits.

3. Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your skin and budget, we’ll recommend a custom plan. This could include:

  • Facials (HydraFacial, oxygen facial, brightening)
  • Injectables (skin boosters, PDRN, BTA, Biostimulators)
  • Lasers (for pigmentation, pores, or scars)
  • Chemical peels
  • Skincare products to support clinic treatments

We’ll walk you through each step clearly and transparently.



After the Consultation: What Happens Next

💡 No Pressure, Just Possibilities

You’ll have all the information to make an informed decision. Many clients choose to start with a trial facial or skin booster on the same day.

📆 Booking Your First Treatment

You can book immediately or take time to consider—it’s up to you. We’ll send your plan via WhatsApp or call for easy reference and reminder.

💌 Follow-up Support

We’ll check in after your first treatment to track progress and answer any post-care questions. We’re here for you long-term, not just for one session.
